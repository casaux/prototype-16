Welcome to myCASA


To access myCASA services you will need to provide your personal details and verify your identity to link your aviation reference number (ARN) to your account.

This will require one of the following documents, so please have one of these ready before continuing:

  • Australian passport
  • Australian birth certificate
  • Australian citizenship certificate
  • Foreign passport
  • ImmiCard
