What is myCASA?

myCASA is an online portal where you can access the following services in myCASA:

  • Apply for an Aviation reference number (ARN)
  • Register drones and RPA's for commercial use
  • Gain commercial accreditation for flying drones and RPA's
  • View and apply for subsequent issues of RPA operator's certificates (ReOCs)

Other online services will be released into myCASA soon.
